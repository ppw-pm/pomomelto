# pomomelto
A productivity timer that turns focus sessions into a refreshing experience. Watch ice melt into your perfect drink as you work, earn stamps for completed sessions, and stay motivated with curated playlists.

## ğŸ¯ How It Works

1. **Set Focus Time** - Choose your session length (25, 45, 60 minutes, or custom)
2. **Start Session** - Your playlist begins and the ice starts melting
3. **Focus & Watch** - The ice gradually melts as time passes
4. **Drink Ready!** - When the ice is fully melted, your drink (and break) is ready
5. **Collect Stamp** - Complete the session and add a stamp to your collection

## ğŸ§Š Why Ice Melting?

Traditional timers can create anxiety with their countdown. Pomomelto uses ice melting because:
- **Calming** - Natural, ambient visualization
- **Rewarding** - Your drink is "ready" when you finish
- **Non-intrusive** - Gentle progress indicator
- **Satisfying** - Watching ice melt is oddly therapeutic!
